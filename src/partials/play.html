How to Play

Match objects and clear the board

  • Find Identical Items

    Search for three matching 3D objects.

  • Clear Puzzle Boards

    Remove all objects to complete levels.

  • Use Helpful Boosters

    Hints and special tools assist during difficult stages.

  • Improve Focus Skills

    Quick recognition helps solve puzzles faster.

  • Unlock New Challenges

    Progress through increasingly detailed puzzle layouts.
